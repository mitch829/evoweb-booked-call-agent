# Allworks Earthworks — Client Knowledge Base

## Owner
- Name: Steven
- Personality: Casual, straight to the point, very approachable and friendly
- Weakness: Slow with follow-ups — needs consistent nudging to stay on top of leads

## Business
- Specialises in retaining walls (20m+) and occasional site cuts
- All leads come from Facebook ads
- Free quotes — no obligation
- Based in Melbourne

## Job Sizes
- Retaining walls: $15,000 – $200,000+
- Site cuts: smaller, faster turnaround
- Most jobs are significant investments for the customer — decisions take time

## Typical Customers
- Homeowners (biggest volume — emotionally invested, price sensitive)
- Builders (professional buyers, move faster, value reliability)
- Developers (larger jobs, slower decisions, multiple quotes)

## Why Leads Don't Go Ahead
- Price is the #1 reason — they get cheaper quotes elsewhere
- Sometimes timing (not ready to start yet)
- Occasionally went with a mate or referral instead
- Rarely: scope changed or project fell through

## Sales Notes
- Steven is genuinely a nice guy — the follow-up texts should reflect that, warm but not pushy
- For large jobs ($50k+) leads often need more time — don't rush them
- For smaller site cuts decisions are faster — follow up sooner
- If price is the objection, Steven can sometimes negotiate or break the job into stages
- Builders and developers respond better to direct, professional language
- Homeowners respond better to reassurance and trust-building

## Tone Guide
- Casual and friendly, not corporate
- Short messages — Steven doesn't write essays
- Encouraging but not desperate
- Never mention competitors
